about Dr.Ali

Hassan Ali, MD, is an internal medicine physician with over 12 years of experience in diagnosing and managing adult medical conditions, including hypertension, diabetes, thyroid disorders, and preventive care. He practices evidence-based medicine and emphasizes effective communication and shared decision-making.
