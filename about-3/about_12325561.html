about An-Najah National University

An-Najah National University is a public university that aims to prepare and develop professional human cadres qualified for leadership in all areas of life. It also aims to provide university students with distinguished scientific knowledge and individual skills that enhance their ability to compete in local, Arab, and international markets, enabling university graduates to become creative and effective individuals. The university also aims to contribute effectively to the advancement of scientific research at the global level, meet the needs of society in the areas of sustainable economic, social, humanitarian, and technological development, contribute to the enrichment of human knowledge, and preserve the cultural and religious heritage of the Palestinian people.

Faculties

The university includes several faculties covering scientific, humanities, and engineering disciplines including:-

  • College of Human Medicine and Allied Medical Sciences
  • College of Engineering
  • College of Honors
  • College of Fine Arts
  • College of Information Technology and Artificial Intelligence
  • College of Nursing

Registration and Admissions

General Admission Requirements:-

  • High School Transcript
  • Personal Photo
  • Birth Certificate

General Admission Requirements:-

  • Application
  • Review
  • Preliminary Acceptance
  • Confirmation of Acceptance by Payment of Fees

Application Deadlines: August 1 to September 30.
