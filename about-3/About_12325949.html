About Dr.Ali

Hasan Ali, MD, is an internal medicine physician with over 12 years of experience in diagnosing and managing adult medical conditions, including hypertension, diabetes, thyroid disorders, and preventive care. He practices evidence-based medicine and emphasizes clear communication and shared decision-making


Dr.Ali at his clinic in Nablus.

  • Thyroid and endocrine disorders
  • Chronic disease management
