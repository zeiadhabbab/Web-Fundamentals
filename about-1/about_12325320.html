About Dr. Hasan Ali

Dr. Hasan Ali is an experienced consultant physician and cardiologist with over 12 years of experience in healthcare. He graduated from Harvard Medical School and completed his residency at Johns Hopkins Hospital, followed by a cardiology fellowship at Mayo Clinic.

Dr. Ali believes in the importance of preventive care and works to educate patients for long-term health improvement.
