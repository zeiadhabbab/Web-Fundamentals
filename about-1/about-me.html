About Dr. Ali

Hasan Ali, MD, is an internal medicine physician with over 12 years of experience in diagnosing and managing adult medical conditions, including hypertension, diabetes, thyroid disorders, and preventive care. He practices evidence-based medicine and emphasizes effective communication and shared decision-making.

Clinical Interests

  • Cardiometabolic health (hypertension, diabetes, dyslipidemia)
  • Preventive medicine and screening
  • Thyroid and endocrine disorders
  • Chronic disease management

Education & Certifications

  • MD (Doctor of Medicine), An-Najah National University Faculty of Medicine — 2011
  • Residency, Internal Medicine, Palestinian Medical Council — 2015
  • Board Certification, Internal Medicine — 2016 (current)
  • Certificate: Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) — Mar 2024

Services
