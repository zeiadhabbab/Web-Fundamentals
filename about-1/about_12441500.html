About Dr. Ali

Hasan Ali, MD, is an Assistant Professor of Internal Medicine at An-Najah National University with over 15 years of experience in teaching and clinical practice. He is dedicated to medical education, mentoring students, and contributing to the development of healthcare in Palestine.

Dr.Hasan Ali at his clinic in Nablus.

Clinical Interests

  • Cardiology
  • Internal Medicine
  • Preventive Care
  • Medical Education

Education & Certification

Dr. Hasan Ali completed his medical degree at Harvard Medical School, followed by a residency in Internal Medicine at Johns Hopkins Hospital. He further specialized in Cardiology through a fellowship at the Mayo Clinic. Currently, he serves as a faculty member at An-Najah National University, where he combines teaching, research, and clinical practice.

Certifications

  • Board Certified in Internal Medicine
  • Board Certified in Cardiology
  • Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS)
  • Basic Life Support (BLS)

Clinical Experience

Dr. Hasan Ali has worked in several leading medical institutions before joining An-Najah National University. He has extensive experience in internal medicine and cardiology and is involved in training future physicians at the university hospital.

Previous Positions

  • Assistant Professor, Faculty of Medicine – An-Najah National University (2018–Present)
  • Attending Physician, An-Najah University Hospital (2018–Present)
  • Cardiology Fellow, Mayo Clinic (2012–2015)
  • Resident, Johns Hopkins Hospital (2009–2012)
